Ruhollah Farchtchi is the founder and CEO of Icebreaker Data. 

Ruhollah is an expert in data systems, most recently leading the AWS Athena engineering team, its fastest growing analytics service. During his time at AWS, he led multiple other data product teams, including: S3 data lake storage; LakeFormation / Glue for data lake catalog; and EMR Spark for advanced, distributed processing of big data. Prior to AWS he was the CTO of startup Zoomdata, a highly innovative BI tool that visualized streaming big data, which was acquired by Insight Software. Ruhollah held multiple customer-facing technical consulting roles, including senior associate at Booz Allen Hamilton.

Ruhollah holds seven patents in information retrieval and data analytics processing and contributes to professional journals. He is a graudate of George Mason University, where he earned a BS in Management Information Systems & Decision Sciences from the Costello Collect of Business, an MS in Information Systems, and is a Candidate for PhD in Information Technology.
